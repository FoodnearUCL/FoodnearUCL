Top 4 favourites for the Meat Lovers

1. Nandos


Nandos stands at the top of the list because they just always get the chicken right and its affordable for students.

Also the nandos on goodge street does a special 10% dicount for students, which makes the meals cheaper.

If you love chicken, peri chips or just want some spicy rice, then Nandos is for you.

Where: 57-59 Goodge St, Fitzrovia, London W1T 1TH

2. Roti King


Roti King is a Malaysian restuuarant around UCL,a good change from the usual.

People flock to Roti King for their rotis; their top-seller, the roti canai special come with two flaky Malaysian flatbreads
that have been freshly made and is served alongside a bowl of curry (chicken, lamb, or veg).

Also affordable for student as the roti cani is only Â£5.50 and other meals within this price range

Where: 40 Doric Way, Euston London

3. Shake Shack


Good burgers are hard to come by but Shake Shack's burger will blow your mind
and it is located just off tottenham court road.

As the saying goes "nothing good comes cheap", it can pricey for everyday lunch but
on those days you have handed in your coursework early and deserve a treat, try their burger along with a shake.

Where: 80 New Oxford StreetLondon, England WC1A 1HB

4. Itsu


Itsu is perfect for quick lunches or dinner. A favourite because it is affordable for students and on those
cold winter days, one of their rice or noodle soup will make you feel better.

Also, 30 minutes before they close their sushi's are half price and there are three in close proximity to UCL,
you don't want to miss it.

Free Wi-Fi available at tottenham court road restaurant

Where: 53 - 54 Tottenham Court Road, Bloomsbury, LONDON, W1T 2EQ.
