Vegan Places

1. Planet Organic

There are two stores in close proximity to UCL and the locations of both are listed below. Where: 22 Torrington Place, London WC1E 7HJ Where: 23-24 Tottenham Court Road, London W1T 1BJ Enjoy freshly prepared soups, smoothies and many other organic produce to try. You can even get your food groceries from both stores.

2. Las Iguanas

It is always difficult to find restaurants that have a vegan tailored menu. Well there is no need to look further as this restaurant provides a vegan tailored menu. This restuarant is located in Brunswick Square. Where: 15-17 Brunswick Square, London WC1N 1AF

3. Ravi Shankar

Enjoy an Indian cuisine at this restaurant. Where: 133-135 Drummond Street, London NW1 2HL

4. Mary Ward Centre Cafe

Enjoy a Spanish tortilla or toasted French-style cheese and mushroom baguette. Where: Bloomsbury 42 Queen's Square, London WC1N 3AQ
